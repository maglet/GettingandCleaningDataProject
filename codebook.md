Codebook: 

General naming scheme:
- t = time
- f = frequency
- body = body motion
- gravity = gravitational motion
- acc = accelerometer
- jerk = signals, derived from accelerometer and angular velocity
- gyro = gyroscope (measures angular velocity)
- mag = magnitude
- mean = mean
- std = standard deviation
- x = x coordinate
- y = y coordinate
- z = z coordinate
